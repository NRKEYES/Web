Au++ nCH4 Energetic Well Summary:

To all involved:

Please find below a comprehensize summary of the energetic wells I have explored for this system.They are orederd in roughly reaction direction, with side products shown along the way. so that we can easily see which structures could correspond to each observed mass. The Y axis, as normal, shows energy in kJ/mol. The energies reported are single point energies with ZPE correction, and the reactants set at 0 kJ/mol.

If you are curious about the structure of one of the shown points, please just click on it! In any modern browser you will see it rendered, and rotated.

Please send any questions or modifications suggestions to me at nkeyes@unm.edu and I will address them ASAP.
